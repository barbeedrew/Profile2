About Me

My name is Drew Barbee and I am really enjoying this class and learning alot. I am originally from Winston-Salem, NC where I lived there for 19 years of my life then moved to Clemson. I used to work at a summer camp outside of Asheville, NC. I moved to Charlotte about 5 years ago just because my best friend said I would enjoy living here. I am very excited and determined to not only finish that class, but to move on to a newer and better life.
